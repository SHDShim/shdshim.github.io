Research profile

S.-H. Dan Shim investigates the behavior of planetary interiors under extreme conditions through experimental and computational methods. His work connects mineral physics to comparative planetology and the broader evolution of planets.

Affiliations

School of Earth and Space Exploration, Materials of the Universe, and FORCE at ASU.
