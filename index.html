Mineral physics and planetary materials under extreme conditions.

We study how materials behave deep inside Earth and other planets using high-pressure experiments, data analysis, and computational tools.

Explore Research Work With Us

Current Focus

Questions that connect minerals to planets.

Deep Earth materials

Elasticity, phase transitions, and transport properties at mantle and core conditions.

Planetary interiors

Interior structure and evolution of rocky planets, icy worlds, and super-Earths.

Open research tools

Reproducible software and analysis workflows for diffraction, equations of state, and teaching.

Research

Core themes

High-pressure experiments

Diamond-anvil cell experiments, synchrotron measurements, and spectroscopy for extreme environments.

Equations of state

Thermodynamic constraints and modeling frameworks for solids, melts, and planetary materials.

Data and code

Custom scientific software for diffraction analysis, fitting, visualization, and reproducible publication.
