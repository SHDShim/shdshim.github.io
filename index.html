Planetary materials and interiors under extreme conditions.

The Dan Shim Lab studies materials at high pressure and high temperature to understand the structure, evolution, and dynamics of Earth and other planets.

Current Lab Focus

Questions that connect materials science to whole planets.

Interiors of rocky and volatile-rich planets

Material properties measured at extreme conditions help constrain how planets form, differentiate, convect, and evolve.

Experimental methods at extreme conditions

The lab combines high-pressure experiments with synchrotron and related techniques to probe mineral and material behavior.

Research, software, and education

Open-source computational tools and teaching materials are part of the lab’s broader effort to connect research and learning.
